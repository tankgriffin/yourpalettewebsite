Master the Korean Spatula Technique

Step-by-step guide to glass skin perfection

1

Prep Your Skin

Apply moisturizer and primer. Let set for 2 minutes. Clean spatula ensures flawless application.

2

Apply Foundation

Place small amounts of foundation on mixing palette or back of hand. Pick up with spatula blade.

3

Gentle Pressure

Use gentle, sweeping motions. The spatula's cool surface helps foundation blend naturally into skin.

4

Build Coverage

Layer gradually for desired coverage. The glass skin effect comes from thin, even layers.
